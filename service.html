LTUMC

I participated in various service opportunities throughout High School, but the majority of my time was spent at my local church Lake Travis United Methodist Church. From 2017 to 2022 I served in various roles within the Audio Visual team. Most of my experience was as a sound technician, doing the audio levels for the band, choir, pastor, speakers, and videos, as well as the setup and takedown. I also assisted with lighting and selecting cameras for the live stream.
